Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ByteNet setup and requirements.

Is ByteNet easy to set up?
Yes! The Windows Installer includes everything you need. Simply run `ByteNet_Setup.exe` and follow the instructions on screen.
What are the minimum system requirements?
ByteNet requires a 64-bit version of Windows 10 or 11, and a screen resolution of at least 1920x1080 for optimal layout display.
How do I activate my subscription?
Simply sign in with your Google account inside ByteNet. Once linked via our Discord or WhatsApp support team, your subscription activates automatically.
