Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about setting up dependencies or requirements? Read our quick guide below.

Does ByteNet require external dependencies?
No! If you use the official Windows Installer, it contains an interactive dependency checker. If Microsoft Visual C++ 2015-2022 Redistributable (x64) is not found, you can choose to have it installed automatically in the background or follow a visual manual setup guide. MPV and Node.js are already pre-packaged in the application.
What are the minimum hardware requirements?
ByteNet requires a minimum screen resolution of 1920x1080 to display the UI properly, and a 64-bit version of Windows 10 or 11. Hardware acceleration decoding is supported and recommended for 4K streams.
Why does ByteNet ask for Administrator privileges?
ByteNet requires administrator permissions to configure local network streaming ports (such as port 45721 and 45722) and automatically set up Windows Firewall rule entries. This allows the backend Node.js WebTorrent modules to accept incoming connections from other torrent peers.
How does licensing work for ByteNet?
ByteNet operates under a Pro License system. Upon installation, you can activate the client by logging in with your Google/Gmail account to unlock high-performance torrent streaming, custom shader controls, and advanced player features.
How do I obtain a ByteNet Pro subscription?
To get a Pro subscription, simply join the official ByteNet Discord Server or our WhatsApp Group. Once joined, our support team will help configure your account subscription and associate it with your Google/Gmail ID.
